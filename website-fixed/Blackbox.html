About Sweat Treats

Sweat Treats is a small, family-owned bakery that has been serving up delicious baked goods for over 20 years. Our cozy shop is the perfect place to relax and enjoy a freshly baked cookie with a cup of coffee.

Cookie Flavors

Our classic chocolate chip cookie is made with premium semi-sweet chocolate chips and a hint of sea salt. A customer favorite!

Our peanut butter cookie is made with creamy peanut butter and a sprinkle of sugar on top. A delicious and satisfying treat.

Our oatmeal raisin cookie is made with rolled oats, plump raisins, and a hint of cinnamon. A hearty and wholesome option.

Our sugarcookie is a classic, buttery cookie topped with sparkling sugar. A simple and delicious treat.
